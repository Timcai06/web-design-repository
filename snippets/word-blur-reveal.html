Word-Level Blur Reveal

来源: lukebaffait.fr · 每个词 blur → clear 独立触发

Every word you see here started as a blur. As you scroll, each one steps into focus — one at a time, at its own pace. This is the same technique used on Luke Baffait's portfolio, where words emerge from a soft blur into sharp clarity. It mimics the way your eyes refocus when you look at something closely. The effect is subtle — most people won't notice the blur at all. They'll just feel like the reading experience is unusually smooth.
